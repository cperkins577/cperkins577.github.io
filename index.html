Cole
Perkins

Born and raised in North Carolina, I graduated from the University of Alabama in 2020 in Physics. Focusing on Astrophysics, I found my passion for programming while writing scripts and programs for my high energy physics labs and I began to pursue a minor in Computer Science alongside Mathematics. Through college, I worked part-time at a local Chipotle in order to pay bills and save money, working on small personal projects and developing my programming skills on the side. Outside of class and after I graduated, I learned Javascript, HTML, CSS, and some React. Now I am focusing on C++ and Unreal Engine, hoping to begin applying all that I have learned to another passion of mine: video games!

I am currently living in Raleigh, North Carolina and working as an Assistant General Manager for a local restraunt. In my spare time I continue to learn and grow in any direction I can. In late 2024 I released my first complete game experience on Itch.io, and have done work involving animation state machines, UI handling, skeleton rigging, and regular gameplay programming, which is my focus. I work in both UE Blueprints and C++.
